### 📖 About me

I work at the intersection of neuroscience and artificial intelligence, applying computational methods and signal processing to physiological data to understand and model the nervous system.

My research focuses on Brain–Computer Interfaces, multimodal biosignal analysis, and the early detection of neuromotor decline.
